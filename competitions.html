Competitions

NJORD

Njord, the Autonomous Ship Challenge is a student driven competition held in Trondheim. It aims to give students the opportunity to challenge themselves and put theory into practice, gaining valuable experience on their journey.


NJORD 2023
Team Aritra secured the Overall 3rd Position in the NJORD 2023 Competition.
Team Aritra also won the "Sustainability Award"
