Who Are We


Team Arita is a Marine Autonomy Competition & Research Team mentored by Prof.Abhilash Sharma, Ocean Engineering & Prof.Atmanand, Former NIOT Director, comprising of undergraduate engineers working with a shared passion on marine robotics.




What We Do


We Design and Build Autonoumous Surface Vessel (ASVS) and develop custom algorithms from the ground up. More than just competing, we're here to learn, innovate, and explore the world of Marine Roboticd through Internation Competetions.
