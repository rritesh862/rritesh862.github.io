About Us

Team Aritra is a team of students from different disciplines in IIT Madras with a shared passion for autonomous marine technology. Every year the team takes up the challenge of learning and building an Autonoumous Surface Vessel, with our own design, software and electrical circuits. We aim to become a Global Competetive Team.
