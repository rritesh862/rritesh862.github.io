Who Are We


Team Arita is a Marine Autonomy Competition & Research Team mentored by Prof.Abhilash Sharma, Ocean Engineering & Prof.Atmanand, Former NIOT Director, comprising of undergraduate engineers working with a shared passion on marine robotics.


Vision


Develop indigenous Autonomous Marine Vessels (ASVs) and (AUVs) for international competitions & research analysis which will have applications in surveillance, seafloor mapping, climate monitoring, passenger ferries, and commercial shipping.


Mision


Our mission is to craft cutting-edge, robust algorithms that will propel our autonomous vessels to triumph on the global stage of prestigious marine autonomy competitions.
